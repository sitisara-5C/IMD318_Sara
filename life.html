My Life

Primary School

I began my education at SK Bandar Baru Kuala Selangor and SRA Bandar Melawati. During my time there, I served as a prefect in both schools and actively participated in extracurricular activities such as volleyball and Kalam Jamaie. These experiences helped me build confidence, discipline, and teamwork skills. It was also during these early years that I discovered my love for learning, especially in subjects that encouraged critical and creative thinking. This stage of my life played an important role in shaping my curiosity and interest in understanding the world around me.

Boarding School Life

After completing my UPSR, I was offered the opportunity to continue my studies at MRSM Felda Trolak. This phase of my life introduced me to friendships that eventually felt like family. I served as a member of the editorial board as a photographer, and in my final year, I was appointed as an LPA, similar to a hostel prefect, as well as the Leader of Multimedia. Living together in a boarding school environment taught me valuable lessons about responsibility, faith, love, and resilience. The memories I built there are ones I will always cherish.

Health Challenge and Change

Although I felt devastated at first, as it was difficult to leave behind a place and people I deeply cared about, Alhamdulillah, this change allowed me to spend more time with my family and focus on my well-being. Currently, I am pursuing my Diploma in Information Management at the Faculty of Information Science, UiTM Rembau. This stage of my journey has been filled with challenges, personal growth, and moments of self-doubt that slowly transformed into confidence.
